Spirit Majic

Clans of the Hidden Axis

In a world where every human carries a dormant Spirit SparC, only those who ignite it through trauma and grief become Spirit-Bound — tethered to one elemental Spirit Animal. The Ankh'Yami Ka'Tet Wujiin are the Hidden Axis, the invisible balance-keepers of a world on the edge of war.

Then Yong' Kheru breaks everything. At seventeen, he awakens bonded to not one, but two Spirit Animals — a golden falcon and a shadow wolf. In recorded history, no such thing has ever occurred. Four elemental nations call him a prophecy. His own clan calls him a threat. He just wants to protect the people he loves.

The Story

Series Overview

The Spirit Majic novel series is the complete written form of the Clans of the Hidden Axis universe — the foundational text from which all other adaptations will grow. Set on the Vael'Koth supercontinent, four elemental nations — Ka'Rai, Mizuchi, Tian'Feng, and Doro — have kept an uneasy peace for centuries, maintained from the shadows by a fifth power no one is supposed to know exists.

"Balance isn't the absence of chaos — it's walking through it without falling."

At its center is Yong' Kheru — a seventeen-year-old who was never supposed to exist the way he does. His dual Spirit Animal bond violates the laws of nature and the laws of politics simultaneously. What follows is a story about identity, inherited power, the cost of prophecy, and what it means to build a family out of the people who stay.

Themes: Privilege & Responsibility · Genetic Fate vs. Choice · Found Family · What Balance Actually Costs.
