The World of
Yun-Ka Teru

In a world where spiritual energy is the ultimate currency, every human carries within them a Spirit SparC — a Spirit-Partition-Resonance-Catalyst. Those who ignite it become Spirit-Bound, tethered to a single Spirit Animal that amplifies their elemental power.

The Vael'Koth supercontinent stretches across the world like a four-pointed star, each arm home to an elemental nation locked in centuries of cold war. At its center lies the Spirit Scar — a glowing crater of ancient power that every nation wants to control.

"The Ankh'Yami Ka'Tet Wujiin are the Hidden Axis — the secret pivot point upon which the world's equilibrium rests."

Hidden from all nations, operating from multi-dimensional sanctuaries, the Ankh'Yami clan stands as the world's invisible balance. They are the jailers of the Spirit Scar. And their chosen heir has just broken every law of nature.

The Mechanics

The Spirit Majic System

Spirit Majic is not a gift. It is the mastery of internal frequency — the biological, neurological, and spiritual architecture that separates the Spirit-Bound from the ordinary world.

⚡

The Spirit SparC

Spirit-Partition-Resonance-Catalyst

The internal engine of all Spirit Majic. Dormant in every human — ignited only by extreme emotional trauma (the Combustion moment). Once lit, the Vertical Circuit activates and a Spirit Animal bond is forged.

🧬

The Vertical Circuit

The Biological Path

Spirit energy flows through three points: the Pineal Ignition (insight, perception), the Spine Conductor (amplification), and the Solar Furnace (output, manifestation). Trauma or Spirit-Blocks cause physical pain along the circuit.

🐾

The Law of the Taming

The Spirit Bond

Every Spirit-Bound must dominate their Spirit Animal through the Willpower Constant. Harmonized flow grants the animal's Frequency. If willpower falters — the animal's primal instinct floods the host, triggering the Vengeful Transition.

⚔️

Six Modes of Manifestation

Combat Framework

Sixth Sense · Physical Enhancement · Elemental Projection · Weapon Manifestation · Domain Expansion · and Full Circuit Mastery. A warrior creates Spirit Majic by manipulating where energy rests within the Vertical Circuit.

🌀

The Twin Horizon Anomaly

The Violation

Yong' Kheru hosts two Spirit Animals within one Vertical Circuit — a biological impossibility. He must maintain two separate Taming constants simultaneously. If one spirit begins the Vengeful Transition, it triggers the other. He would become a Dual-Revenant — a Tier-0 threat capable of leveling mountain ranges.

🔮

Circuit Scarring (Zenkai)

Growth Through Damage

When a Spirit-Bound's circuit is severely damaged and heals, it rebuilds stronger. This Zenkai Capability — growth through breaking — is what separates legends from warriors. The wound remembers. The spirit ascends.
