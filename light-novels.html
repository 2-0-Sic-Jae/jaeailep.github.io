The light novel adaptation of the Spirit Majic universe — faster-paced, character-forward, with in-world illustrations embedded throughout. Each volume covers one half of a major story arc, built for readers who live inside characters' heads.

250–350 pages per volume. 10–12 chapters each. 8–12 original illustrations per volume, plus bonus content: character profiles, world lore deep-dives, and side stories that don't appear in the main novel series.
